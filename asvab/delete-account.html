Account deletion

Delete Account - ASVAB Prep

You can delete your ASVAB Prep account and associated personal app data from inside the app or by contacting MKB Digital from the email address linked to your account.

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Fast path Use Profile > Delete account in the app.

Email support is available if you cannot access the app or need help with the deletion request.

1. How to delete your account

You can request deletion in either of these ways:

  • In the app: open ASVAB Prep, go to Profile, and choose Delete account.
  • By email: send a request to devmkbapps0@gmail.com from the email address used for your ASVAB Prep account.

If you email us, use the subject line ASVAB Prep account deletion. We may ask for additional information to verify that you own the account before deleting data.

2. What will be deleted or anonymized

After a verified deletion request, we will delete or anonymize personal data associated with your account, including:

  • Account profile data and app preferences
  • Study progress, XP, streaks, ranks, badges, and daily objectives
  • Practice history, question logs, wrong answers, favorites, notes, and saved questions
  • Mock exam and mini exam attempt history
  • AI mentor messages and related study context linked to your account, where applicable
  • Push notification tokens and app notification records linked to your account

3. What may be retained

Some limited records may be retained where necessary for legal, security, payment, tax, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, store compliance, or backup integrity reasons. Retained records are limited to what is reasonably needed for those purposes.

Backup copies may take additional time to expire from managed systems. We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days unless a longer period is required by law or platform rules.

4. Subscriptions and store purchases

Deleting your ASVAB Prep account does not automatically cancel an active Apple App Store or Google Play subscription. Subscriptions, refunds, receipts, billing history, renewals, and cancellations are managed by the store where you made the purchase.

  • For Apple purchases, manage or cancel the subscription in your Apple account settings.
  • For Google Play purchases, manage or cancel the subscription in your Google Play account settings.

5. Contact

For account deletion help, email devmkbapps0@gmail.com.
