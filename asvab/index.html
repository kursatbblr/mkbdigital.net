FAQ

Does ASVAB Prep work offline?

Some study content may be available offline after loading, but account sync, AI mentor, payments, and notifications require an internet connection.

How does the AI mentor work?

It can explain concepts, break down mistakes, and suggest what to review. It may be wrong, so official sources and your own judgment remain important.

How can I cancel a subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple App Store or Google Play, depending on where the purchase was made. Cancellation rules follow that store's terms.

Which languages are supported?

The app experience is designed around English ASVAB preparation content.
